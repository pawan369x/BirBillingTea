Our Story & Heritage

THE BIR COOPERATIVE
TEA FACTORY LTD.

Nestled near the bustling Bir Bazaar Road, amidst the serene landscapes of the Kangra Valley, stands our pride. Surrounded by mist-kissed tea gardens and the majestic Dhauladhar range, we are a living testament to Himachal's rich tea heritage.

We preserve the time-honored art of crafting authentic Kangra Tea. By exclusively supporting and processing leaves from local small-scale growers, every cup empowers the community. From traditional withering processes to our live guided factory tours, we offer visitors an unblemished taste of Bir's natural essence.
